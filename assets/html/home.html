How Loopring Works?

  • Loopring works by letting users create orders from their own wallets. These orders are signed with private keys and do not require deposits or transfers.

  • The orders are then shared in a dedicated consortium blockchain to build customizable order-books to identify tradable rings.

  • The ring settlements are completed on the public blockchain, which guarantees atomic transfers of tokens, higher trading speeds, and zero-risk.
